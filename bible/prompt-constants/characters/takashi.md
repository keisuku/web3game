# キャラクター固定プロンプト — タカシ（スピンオフ「脱サランナー」主人公）

> スピンオフ専用キャラ。翔一とは別人で、外見が混同されないよう細部を明確に肯定描写で分ける。
> 画像生成モデルは否定文を素材として拾うため、英語記述は**肯定文のみ**で構成する。

## strict-rules（タカシが登場するページで強制適用）

```
Takashi character consistency (all panels of this page):
- 32-year-old Japanese man, slim lean frame, a younger salaryman appearance.
- Round thin-wire black glasses with a circular frame outline and a thin rim (clearly a different shape from the rectangular-frame glasses of other characters).
- Short slightly messy pure black hair throughout (no grey mixed in).
- The same face shape, same glasses frame, same hairstyle, and same age impression in every panel.
- Glasses remain on the face in every panel.
- Wardrobe follows the panel description: dark business suit on commute pages, casual jogging gear on running pages, contemplative wear on decision pages.
- This is a solo spinoff starring Takashi; the AI character "Kuro-chan" does not appear in Takashi's pages.
```

## base（すべてのモードに前置）

```
Character: Takashi, a 32-year-old Japanese male software engineer. Round thin-wire black glasses with a circular frame outline. Short slightly messy pure black hair. Slim build, lean face, a mildly fatigued baseline expression. Grounded semi-realistic manga style, ordinary everyday face, consistent across every panel.
```

## fatigue（疲弊モード・P01 導入）

```
Dark business suit with a loose tie. Standing in a packed morning commuter train, hand gripping an overhead strap. Dark circles under the eyes. Downward blank gaze with natural pupil size keeping small highlights, mouth closed, shoulders slumped.
```

## curious-phone（スマホ検索モード・P01）

```
Bust shot, slight lean toward the phone screen. Right hand holding a smartphone at a 10-15 degree tilt with cyan screen glow reaching the face. Eyebrows slightly raised, mouth barely parted. The earlier fatigue still present, replaced by a small spark of interest.
```

## atm-transfer（ATM送金モード・P02）

```
Bust shot, standing in front of a convenience-store ATM at night. Inserting a debit card. Slight tension in the fingers. Glasses lenses reflect the ATM screen glow (clean white light). Mouth tight.
```

## first-run（初ランモード・P03）

```
Full body running shot in an early morning park. White T-shirt, navy track jacket unzipped, black running shorts, plain white sneakers labelled "Walker". Loose posture rather than athletic. A smartphone in an arm strap emits a cyan screen glow. Breath visible in the cool air.
```

## discovery-smile（発見の微笑・P03 climax）

```
Close-up face. Eyes focused on a smartphone screen held at a 10-15 degree tilt; the cyan glow lights half the face. A small wondering smile raises the corner of the mouth. Glasses lenses reflect the +5 GST counter on the screen.
```

## analyst-mode（分析モード・P04）

```
Seated at a cluttered home desk with a laptop open showing a spreadsheet. Multiple smartphones resting on the desk. Right hand on the mouse, left hand touching the chin. Glasses lenses reflect the screen light. Eyes calm and focused. A faint smirk of mathematical satisfaction.
```

## climax-decision（決意モード・P05）

```
Silhouette-heavy bust shot in an office meeting room. Only the corner of the mouth and the glasses rim visible. Strong backlight through the window behind him. Very still posture. Serious but calm expression — a person who has just chosen their own life.
```

## back-view-free（自由モード後ろ姿・P06）

```
Back view. Walking away down a weekday city street at midday. No suit this time — wearing a T-shirt, light jacket, jeans, and sneakers. A small duffel bag over the shoulder. One hand in the pocket. Face turned slightly over the shoulder, with only the single visible eye through the glasses. Expression composed — the face of someone who has just become free.
```
